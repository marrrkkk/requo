# GreenEdge Landscaping — Business Knowledge

## About Us

GreenEdge Landscaping is a family-owned landscaping and garden maintenance business based in Brisbane, Australia. We've been operating since 2018 and serve residential and small commercial properties across the north side suburbs (Chermside, Stafford, Kedron, Nundah, Wavell Heights, and surrounding areas).

## Service Area

We service properties within a 20 km radius of our base in Stafford. Travel outside this zone incurs a $25 call-out fee. We do not service properties south of the Brisbane River.

## Operating Hours

- Monday to Friday: 7:00 AM – 4:30 PM
- Saturday: 7:30 AM – 12:00 PM (by appointment only)
- Sunday and public holidays: Closed

Emergency storm damage call-outs are available after hours at 1.5x standard rates.

## Booking and Cancellation Policy

- Bookings require 48 hours notice for scheduling.
- Cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment incur a $40 cancellation fee.
- No-shows are charged the full quoted amount.
- Rain delays are rescheduled to the next available slot at no extra charge.

## Payment Terms

- Residential one-off jobs: payment due on completion (card, bank transfer, or cash).
- Recurring maintenance plans: invoiced fortnightly, payment due within 7 days.
- Commercial contracts: invoiced monthly, net 14 days.
- Late payments incur a $15 admin fee after 14 days overdue.

## Equipment and Safety

We carry full public liability insurance ($20M) and WorkCover for all team members. Our equipment is commercial-grade and maintained weekly. We supply all tools, fuel, and consumables — customers do not need to provide anything.

## Green Waste Disposal

All green waste is removed from site unless the customer requests it be left for composting. Standard removal is included for maintenance plans. One-off jobs include one trailer load; additional loads are charged at $75 per load including tip fees.

## Quoting Process

- We provide free on-site quotes for jobs over $200.
- Smaller jobs can be quoted via photos sent through our inquiry form.
- Quotes are valid for 30 days from issue date.
- Prices may vary from the quote if site conditions differ significantly from what was described or photographed.

## Recurring Plan Benefits

Customers on a recurring maintenance plan receive:
- 10% discount on all one-off add-on services
- Priority scheduling (guaranteed same-day rain reschedule)
- Free annual irrigation system check (valued at $110)
- Quarterly property health report with recommendations

## Seasonal Notes

- **Summer (Dec–Feb):** High demand period. Book 2 weeks ahead for one-off work. Mowing frequency increases to weekly for most lawns.
- **Autumn (Mar–May):** Best time for lawn aeration, overseeding, and garden bed renovation.
- **Winter (Jun–Aug):** Reduced mowing frequency. Good time for hard pruning and structural planting.
- **Spring (Sep–Nov):** Fertiliser applications, mulching, and pre-summer garden prep. Our busiest booking period for makeovers.

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Do I need to be home?**
A: No. As long as we have gate/access instructions, we can complete the work while you're out. We'll send a completion photo via text.

**Q: What happens if it rains?**
A: We reschedule to the next available slot at no charge. Maintenance plan customers get same-day priority rebooking.

**Q: Can you remove a large tree?**
A: We handle pruning and small tree removal (under 6m). For large removals requiring crane access or arborist certification, we refer to our partner arborist and can coordinate on your behalf.

**Q: Do you do landscaping design?**
A: We focus on maintenance and garden renovation rather than full landscape architecture. For new builds or major redesigns, we can recommend a landscape designer and then handle the ongoing maintenance once installed.
